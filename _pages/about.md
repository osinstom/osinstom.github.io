My name is Tomek. I’m the SDN/NFV enthusiast. My R&D work is related to network softwarization including network automation & orchestration, Software-Defined Infrastructure (i.a. SDN), Network Function Virtualization (NFV), Network Slicing, IoT, 5G networks and Telco Cloud technologies. Recently, I am involved in the projects related to the P4 technology and OpenStack. I have contributed to various open-source projects including Floodlight, ONOS and ONAP.

Professionally, I work as Principal R&D Specialist at Orange Labs Poland. Moreover, I am a PhD candidate at Warsaw University of Technology and the member of SDN and Network Virtualization Lab (www.snvlab.tele.pw.edu.pl).

On this blog I would like to share reports, tutorials and news about SDN/NFV technologies and popularize network softwarization.
