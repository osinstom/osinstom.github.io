I'm **Tomek Osiński**, a SDN/NFV enthusiast and R&D Software Engineer. My R&D activities are related to network softwarization including network automation & orchestration, Software-Defined Infrastructure (i.a. SDN), Network Function Virtualization (NFV), Network Slicing, mobile networks and Telco Cloud technologies. Recently, I took interest in low-level programming and research on data plane programmability for SDN and NFV systems. Currently, I'm involved in the project related to the P4 technology and OpenStack. I have been contributing to various open-source projects including Floodlight, ONOS and ONAP.

Professionally, I work as Principal R&D Specialist at Orange Labs Poland. Moreover, I am a PhD candidate at Warsaw University of Technology and the member of SDN and Network Virtualization Lab ([www.snvlab.tele.pw.edu.pl](www.snvlab.tele.pw.edu.pl)).

I would like to share demos, reports, tutorials and news about SDN/NFV technologies that I will find interesting.
