title: Different ways to match packets..

One a year I give a lecture at Warsaw University of Technology presenting the P4 language and the idea of programmable switches. I recognized that, almost every time, students have a problem to understand different matching procedures that can be used in the packet processing devices. In this blog post, I try to cover different ways to match packets in the Match-Action pipelines and what algorithms are used there.
